Fabrice Kntumba is an accomplished saxophonist based in Windhoek, specializing in elegant and engaging background music for a variety of social events, including gala dinners, weddings, and corporate functions.

Fabrice's music is a journey through a diverse repertoire of jazz standards, smooth jazz, blues, and captivating Latin melodies. This variety ensures a sophisticated and enjoyable atmosphere for any occasion. He is a recognized talent in Namibia, with a track record of performances for prestigious clients such as the University of Namibia, Namibian Broadcasting Corporation, The Village Garden, and The Sky Restaurant. His past clients also include Green Hydrogen Namibia, Ilo Events, and Crescendo Namibia. For a taste of his recent work, check out his YouTube video titled "my worship."
