Fabrice Kntumba is an accomplished saxophonist based in Windhoek, specializing in elegant and engaging background music for a variety of social events, including gala dinners, weddings, and corporate functions.

BOOKINGS

Fabrice's music is a journey through a diverse repertoire of jazz standards, smooth jazz, blues, and captivating Latin melodies. This variety ensures a sophisticated and enjoyable atmosphere for any occasion. He is a recognized talent in Namibia, with a track record of performances for prestigious clients such as the University of Namibia, Namibian Broadcasting Corporation, The Village Garden, and The Sky Restaurant.
